Boolean Values

Are essential in these comparisons. With each decision we need the computer to make, we eventually answer in yes or no manner -- JavaScript, yes or no is represented by:

  • true
  • false

  1. 5 == 4 prints: false

    5 is not equal to 4

  2. 5 > 4 prints: true

    5 is greater than 4

  3. 5 < 4 prints: false

    5 is not less than 4

  4. 5 >= 4 prints: true

    5 is greater than 4 even though it is not equal to 4

  5. 5 <= 4 prints: false

    5 is not less than or equal to 4

  6. 5 > 4 && 4 > 2 prints: true

    both statements have to be true and 5 is greater than 4, likewise 4 is greater than 2

  7. 4 > 10 || 1 == 1 prints: true

    only one statement has to be true 4 is not greater than 10, but 1 is equal to 1
